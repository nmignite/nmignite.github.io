Narsee Monjee Institute of Technology and Management has a rich and long-standing legacy of forging collaborations and building partnerships with the industry and the corporates while providing an excellent education to mentor the students into an industry-ready workforce.
NMIMS has established itself through opportunistic ideologies, and believes in providing its students with a plethora of enriching experiences to expose its students to new opportunities and inhibit a multi-lateral mindset so as to provide the most compound results for the companies they work for.

The NMIMS Navi Mumbai Campus is situated in a rapidly growing educational hub of Kharghar in Navi Mumbai - in a city where thousands of students are willing to participate in such a tech-fest, making it a perfect opportunity to allow students to feed their curiosity.

Techfests are shown to be really popular in attendance among students regardless of their educational specialisation, who are visibly livid and exuberantly passionate about technologies that enthral their senses. Students find it enriching and enchanting to immerse themselves in virtual and real-world technological games while simultaneously remaining keen to expand their lexicons and knowledge by attending workshops.
Techfests provide a perfect window for students to learn and be inspired with fun and games.

IGNITE 2019 is our attempt to generate interest and showcase opportunities to the students in an entertaining and enriching way by offering riveting gameplay and knowledge enhancing programs.
